Our Info

Step into the heart of Koufu, where our beverage stall invites you to savor the best in every sip. Immerse yourself in a world of flavors crafted just for you. Our baristas will brew up the perfect cup of coffee for you — rich, bold, and aromatic. Whether you prefer coffee or tea, our menu boasts an array of choices to suit every taste. Indulge in our drinks, each one a carefully curated blend of ingredients designed to delight your palate. Come, sip, and experience the joy of good company, delightful beverages, and delicious breakfast offerings.
